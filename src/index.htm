COVID19 Infographic - Spain

by Colin Clark

The Issue

from BBC.com

  • Spain has a Higher Case Count than Any Other European Country
  • Although the Younger Citizens of the Country are More Immune to the Virus, They are Its Primary Carrier
  • Healthcare Coverage Varies Widely Among Different Regions of the Country and is Especially Poor for Migrant Workers

The Economic Impact

from ElPais.com

  • Spain's GDP had the 17th Largest Worldwide Decrease
  • The Country's Unemployment is Predicted to Drop as Far as 17%
  • The Bank of Spain Expects a Drop of 13% in GDP for the Coming Year

Restrictions

from Spain.info

  • All Visitors and Immigrants are Required to Pass a Health Check Before Being Granted Entry
  • As of Now, People are Unable to Travel Outside of Their Residential Region (Andalucia, Catalonia, etc.)
  • There is a Mandatory Curfew Enforced Along with a Restriction of How Late Most Businesses are Allowed to Operate
  • Everyone Over the Age of Six is Required to Wear a Mask

Fiscal Policies

from home.kmpg

  • Although Workers May be Laid Off, They Will Receive Government Sponsored Paid-Leave
  • The Government will Ensure Liquidity to Help Stimulate Economic Activity
  • Spanish Industries Engaged in International Trade will be Backed by the Government
  • The VAT Tax will be Alleviated for the Importation of Goods for Emergency Victims
